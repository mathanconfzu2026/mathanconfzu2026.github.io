About the conference

We warmly invite you to take part in the International Scientific Conference “Modern Problems of Mathematical Analysis and Function Theory”, which will be held on 24–25 September 2026 at the Department of Mathematical Analysis, Business Analytics and Statistics, Faculty of Physics and Mathematics, Zhytomyr Ivan Franko State University.

The conference will be organised in a hybrid format: on-site sessions on 24 September and online sessions via Zoom on 25 September. Participants coming from other cities can be accommodated in the university dormitory.

Working languages: Ukrainian and English.

Participation in the conference is free of charge. All accepted contributions will be published in the electronic conference proceedings, and participants will receive electronic certificates of participation together with the proceedings.

Please note that contributions must respect academic integrity. Papers that violate copyright or academic ethics will not be accepted. The organisers do not cooperate with researchers affiliated with the aggressor state or those who support it.

Topics

The conference aims to bring together researchers working in modern analysis and related areas, including (but not limited to):

  • Real and complex analysis
  • Extremal problems in analysis and function theory
  • Hypercomplex analysis
  • Statistical modelling
  • Problems of mathematical physics
  • Probability theory and mathematical statistics

Submission guide

We welcome original results of theoretical or experimental character that have not been published previously.

  • The length of the abstract is up to 2 pages (including references).
  • Abstracts should be prepared in LaTeX and submitted both as .tex and .pdf files.
  • Please use the official conference LaTeX template: see the download link below.
  • The reference list should appear at the end of the abstract in the order of citation.
  • All submissions will undergo a plagiarism check. In case of excessive borrowing, authors may be asked to revise the text or the contribution can be rejected.

File naming convention:

  • Registration_Surname
  • Abstract_tex_Surname
  • Abstract_pdf_Surname

Download LaTeX template

The template also contains a short example showing how to format theorems, equations, and the reference list.
