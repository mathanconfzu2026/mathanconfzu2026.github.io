About the conference

The Department of Mathematical Analysis, Business Analytics and Statistics of Zhytomyr Ivan Franko State University invites you to participate in the International Conference on Mathematical Analysis, Mapping Theory and their Applications.

The conference topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Mathematical analysis and function theory;
  • Mappings with controlled/finite distortion;
  • Differential equations and dynamical systems;
  • Probabilistic methods and their applications;
  • Applied problems of analysis and modelling.

Working languages: Ukrainian and English.
