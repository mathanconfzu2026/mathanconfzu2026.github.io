About the conference

We invite you to participate in the І International Scientific Conference “Modern Problems of Mathematical Analysis and Function Theory”, which will be held on September 24-25, 2026 at the Department of Mathematical Analysis, Business Analysis and Statistics of Zhytomyr Ivan Franko State University. The conference will be held in a hybrid format.

Forms of participation: On-site session on September 24; online session (Zoom) on September 25.

Working languages: Ukrainian, English.

Submission guide

We welcome original results of theoretical or experimental character that have not been published previously.

  • The length of the abstract is up to 2 pages (including references).
  • Abstracts should be prepared in LaTeX and submitted both as .tex and .pdf files.
  • Please use the official conference LaTeX template: see the download link below.
  • The reference list should appear at the end of the abstract in the order of citation.
  • All submissions will undergo a plagiarism check. In case of excessive borrowing, authors may be asked to revise the text or the contribution can be rejected.

File naming convention:

  • Registration_Surname
  • Abstract_tex_Surname
  • Abstract_pdf_Surname

Download LaTeX template

The template also contains a short example showing how to format theorems, equations, and the reference list.
